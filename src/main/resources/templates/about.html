Why did we create Fast Learning?

Programming, coding and languages are just a few of the topics a Computer Science program student will learn while in school. Students can be overwhelmed with the concepts and feel the need to turn to additional resources to help compliment study routines. Searching the internet can yield multiple results without taking into consideration the quality and adequacy of what is being posted. With so many results, the student now needs to comb through multiple finds and try to find one that is fairly recent and aligns to the class topics.

Why is Fast Learning different?

We provide a student focused platform that provides the best resources aligned to course content to help students excel in class. A platform that is built differently than other educational aid sites because of the partnership between users and contributors. This application provides students a place to search specific courses and instructors to quickly find reliable sources to study with and excel in school. This offers users a student support that is geared towards the right type of help students need and allows them to focus on studying and not hunting for resources.
